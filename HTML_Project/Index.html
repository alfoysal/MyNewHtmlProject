Welcome To ABC Transport

ABC Transport is apparently a family owned transport company specializing in transportation of passenger bus services. From a humble beginning of local services, our transport system encompasses Dhaka -Chattogram-Cox's Bazar routes.

We worked hard and honest, we put our vision forward and explored ways and means to continuously improve passenger comfort. We take pride in mentioning that our fleet of buses includes the most luxurious models of VOLVO and SCANIA Imported from Europe, which provide the ultimate in passenger comfort and safety.

The company at present operates more then 20 (Twinty) buses on schedule routes employing over 90 trained staff .
